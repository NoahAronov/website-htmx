About
Noah is a university student at a National Center of Academic Excellence in Cyber Defense (CAE-CD), studying for a Master of Science, Cybersecurity and Information Assurance degree. He also holds a Bachelor of Science, Cybersecurity and Information Assurance degree. During university studies he has obtained over a dozen industry-recognized certifications and has completed over three hundred hands-on practical labs in information technology and information security. Noah has been a volunteer and member of the university Cybersecurity Club, providing graphic design and technical documentation for club events.
Noah has knowledge and hands-on experience in many areas of information technology from networking to cloud computing. For security, some of his topics of interest include: security engineering, network security, cloud security, application security, container security, security architecture, penetration testing, cyber threat intelligence, red team infrastructure and development, security research, and cyber operations. Future certifications that he is interested in studying for mostly align with offensive security and cloud computing. Noah is also studying mathematics, physics, and engineering to work with radio and electronic devices.
Noah has over ten thousand hours of research in global security studies using primary sources from academic, military, and research institutions such as NATO and DARPA. These studies have focused on emerging and developing technologies (EDTs) and cognitive warfare. Each of these is a framework for conceptualizing and strategizing with hundreds of technologies across over a dozen academic domains. Some elements of cybersecurity and information security are considered when studying EDTs and cognitive warfare, especially social engineering and cyber threat intelligence. Noah has used over one thousand documents, scientific journal articles, books, white papers, and interviews with leading experts to shape his global security expertise over more than five years.
Noah has over five years of experience in customer service and customer experience, with the vast majority of that time spent as a team leader, trainer, and advisor to the business owner. He was the assistant head chef and lead chef trainer for over three years at multiple restaurants, training over ten junior chefs in a traditional culinary art. As a leader he was also directly responsible for compliance with health and safety regulations, inventory management, team development, and customer experience. Noah has also advised two executives and four business owners in business strategy such as marketing, product development, design, research, and transportation.
Noah has been a state regional finalist competitive swimmer, having participated in two high school swim teams and two club swim teams in two states; he has backpacked to the top of Mount Whitney, the tallest mountain in the continental United States; he has been snowboarding over a dozen times in the Sierra Nevadas; and Noah has also pursued various martial arts using historical weapons, including swords and polearms from Europe, the Middle East, and Asia.
